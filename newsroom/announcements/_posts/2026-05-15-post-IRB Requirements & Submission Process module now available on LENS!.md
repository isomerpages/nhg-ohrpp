<p>The new ‘<strong>IRB Requirements &amp; Submission Process’ </strong>module
is now available on<strong> LENS</strong>  <em>(Accessible to NHG Health Staff)</em>
</p>
<p>This<strong> e-learning </strong>module was jointly developed by NHG Health
and SingHealth to familiarise researchers with IRB requirements for preparing
and submitting study applications to the NHG Health Domain Specific Review
Board (DSRB) or SingHealth Centralised Institutional Review Board (CIRB)
as applicable via the ECOS System.</p>
<p><em><u>Module Learning Objectives:</u></em>
</p>
<p>•&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; Evaluate research activities
to determine IRB requirements and prepare appropriate pre-submission documentation.</p>
<p>•&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; Understand the roles and responsibilities
of researchers and Institution.</p>
<p>•&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; Understand the IRB application
process.</p>
<p>•&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; Understand the different IRB
review categories and potential IRB review outcomes.</p>
<p>•&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; Manage post-approval monitoring
and reporting requirements and study closure procedures.</p>
<p><em><u>How to access:</u></em>
</p>
<p>•&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; <strong>NHG Health Staff</strong> may
self-register to access the <strong>module </strong>on <a href="https://lens.nhghealth.com.sg" rel="noopener nofollow" target="_blank">LENS</a>
</p>
